About Our School

Madrasa Al-Noor is dedicated to providing exceptional Islamic education that combines traditional Islamic teachings with modern academic excellence. Our mission is to develop well-rounded Muslim students who are confident in their faith, excel academically, and contribute positively to society.

Our Islamic Educational Philosophy

We believe that true education encompasses both worldly knowledge and spiritual development. Our curriculum integrates Quranic teachings, Hadith studies, and Islamic values with contemporary subjects, ensuring our students are prepared for both this life and the hereafter.

Our History

Founded with the vision of providing authentic Islamic education, Madrasa Al-Noor has been serving the Muslim community for decades. Our journey reflects our commitment to excellence in both religious and academic education.

1985

Foundation

Madrasa Al-Noor was established by a group of dedicated community members with just 25 students in a small rented building.

1992

First Hafiz Graduation

Our first student completed Hifz, marking the success of our Quran memorization program.

1998

New Campus

Moved to our current purpose-built campus with modern facilities including library, computer lab, and prayer hall.

2005

Accreditation

Received official accreditation, allowing our graduates to pursue higher education seamlessly.

2015

500 Graduates

Celebrated our 500th graduate, with alumni serving communities worldwide as teachers, doctors, engineers, and Islamic scholars.

2023

Digital Learning

Launched online learning platforms and digital resources while maintaining our traditional teaching excellence.
